Keeping Up With Gen Z

Brainrot

It is scary to think that the youth of today are one day going to be running our world. At the rate they're going, by 2055 we will be travelling to the 'Leaning Tower of Sigma' and the 'Alpha Wall of China.' Many parents are struggling to connect with their children, and one of the reasons for this is the language barrier. So this is all you need to know about the trending language of Gen Z and Alpha, also known as Brainrot.

Alpha and Beta

To be known as the alpha is linked to the recent outburst of 'furries' who transform themselves into the 'alpha wolf.' To be the Alpha is a good thing, it means a popular and dominant leader, but also a lone, independent wolf. To be a beta is the complete opposite, it is someone who is shy and submissive, weak or lacking in confidence. So basically, alpha=good, beta=bad.

Skibidi

Skibidi gained popularity from the viral "Skibidi Toilet" youtube shorts created with a storyline around human-headed toilets. Skibidi does not have any proper meaning, it can basically be used as an adjective anywhere, but it does usually have a negative and cringe connotation to it. Example: "Thats so skibidi" or "what the skibidi?"

Aura

Aura has basically the same meaning as we all know: the vibe that someone gives off. But Gen Alpha has managed to twist it to become a foundational word in the Brainrot dictionary. If you do something embarassing or awkward, like trip down the stairs they will say that is "minus" or "negative aura". But doing something really cool like catching a ball one handed from across the field, with minimal effort and excitement will give you "plus 10 000 aura."

Mewing/Mog/Looksmaxxing

These all refer to your physical appearance. Mewing is a technique of flattening your tongue against the roof of your mouth which enhances your jawline, which has become very popular in Gen Z and Alpha. To "mog" means to look more attractive than someone else. Looksmaxxing means to enhance and maximise your appearance, by skincare, dieting or even plastic surgery. So a "Looksmax Mogger" would be someone who has reached their full appearance potential.

 

I am not saying you have to start using this Brainrot language, but it may be useful to understand what your children are saying. Soon these words will join "slay" and "yeet" in the forgotten words of past generations. But until then, you will be able to translate your children, and everyone will know you're the alpha.
